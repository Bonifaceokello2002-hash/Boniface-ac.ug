About me

Building the web, one community at a time.

I'm Boniface, a web designer and developer based in Uganda. My work sits at the intersection of design and access — I want the schools, churches, NGOs and small businesses that never thought a website was "for them" to have one that actually works.

Every site I build is responsive, fast, and designed around what the visitor is actually trying to do — whether that's a parent calculating school fees or a member reading FAQs before their first visit.

Mission

To provide innovative, responsive, and affordable website solutions that help businesses, schools, churches, NGOs, and individuals establish a strong online presence and achieve their digital goals through quality web design and reliable support.

Vision

To become one of the leading and most trusted web design professionals in Uganda and beyond — recognised for creativity, innovation, quality, and excellent customer service.
